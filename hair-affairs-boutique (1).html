Our Story

The Art of
Hair Locking

At Hair Affairs Boutique, the art of hair locking is taken to new heights, catering to everyone from children to adults with a passion for Sisterlocks® and traditional locks.

The salon offers a range of services, ensuring each client leaves feeling revitalised and confident. With a commitment to healthy hair care, the boutique provides a nurturing environment perfect for maintaining or beginning your loc journey.

Founded by Michelle, Master Sisterloctician, our team brings years of expertise and genuine passion to every appointment — because your hair deserves nothing less.
